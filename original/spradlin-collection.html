Story of Jarred Daniel Spradlin

What makes a good man? Is it the amount of kind acts he does everyday, or is the love he has for people? Jarred Daniel Spradlin was more than just a good man. Jarred touched the lives of so many people by just being his loving and outgoing self. It was rare to ever see Jarred upset. The only thing you would notice was his lit up smile or his green lit eyes. Everywhere he went people felt loved. They felt noticed and more importantly they felt apart of something. Jarred had a strong passion for people. Never once did he turn his shoulder or look past someone because they were different. He was the guy that would remember your name and made sure that every time he saw you he would stop and say hello and give a hug.

On Friday April 18, 2014 Jarred Daniel Spradlin left this Earth to be in paradise in heaven. Although his time on earth was cut short, his work was not finished. Jarred holds the highest role in this business because he is the heart of Bingo 93. His inspiring will and love to help others has migrated to the hearts of many and will continue to do so. In honor of Jarred's life, Bingo 93 has created a special collection known as, The Spradlin Collection. The love for people and the enthusiasm he shared will continue throughout this business and touch every heart that comes in contact with. Jarred was more than a hero, but a legend, and legends never die. Jarred is now watching over us now, continuing to make sure everyone gets noticed, just like he always did. With this collection it will allow people to remember to love, hope and strive for their dreams. That is what Jarred would have wanted.
