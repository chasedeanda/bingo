Statement

Bingo 93 comes from the game bingo. You go and pick the card that you believe will win, but you will always have people telling you to pick this card because I want that one you have or pick this card because it's a winner. In reality you want to make the decision for yourself. Now change that into a life perspective. The card is your life, but you will always have people telling you to do this because that's what's best for you or it's the comfortable route or you were born in the streets grown up in the streets and that's all you know. In your head you want to follow your dream. A dream as far as being a musician an artist a doctor anything you want to be. At Bingo 93, our slogan is, "Choose Your Fate," choose the card of lifestyle you wish to live and become great at it. Our mission at Bingo 93 is to find those people and let them know that there is still hope in doing what they have a passion for the numbers 9 & 3 come from Thelma and Agape which mean Will and Love. The family at Bingo 93 are willing to go the extra mile to find those people and give them more exposure by creating events, using social media, or even sponsoring. We do all this to help people live their dream and not settle for less because we love them. There is still love in humanity and their are still people who care about others and only want to see them succeed. At Bingo 93 we want to see people reach their dreams and we want nothing more than to express our will and love.

"It's the possibility of having a dream come true, that is what makes life interesting."

Apparel With Purpose

Each style of clothing made represents a tone, a different region within each of us. Our goal is to make everyone feel confident and comfortable in our threads. We're here to motivate you to choose your own fate. Find your future and run with it!

Outreach & Community

We want to give back to the community. Sometimes everyone needs help, even if it's little by little. We want to inspire people through example to strive for their dreams. Life is what you make it. Give yourself a reason to live.

Breaking Barriers

Bingo's main goal is to make a difference and break the status quo. Our mission is to not only become a future sponsor, but to also create a future family that desires to help others by never settling. Choose your fate and work for your ambition. Nothing in life comes simply, but anything in this world is possible.
